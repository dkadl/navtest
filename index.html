About This Website

We empower urban seniors in Bengaluru to live more independently through smarter solid waste management.

Rapid urbanization and the shift to nuclear families have left many seniors managing alone. Unreliable door-to-door collections, hard-to-find bins, and unclear information often turn everyday tasks into sources of stress and isolation.

That’s why we built SWM — a free, easy-to-use web platform with an interactive GIS map. By integrating municipal data, it delivers real-time visibility into collection schedules, bin locations, and service status. Seniors can quickly locate bins, report issues, track pickups, and manage their waste with confidence and autonomy.

Our mission is simple: reduce missed or delayed collections, eliminate daily hassles, and support thousands of seniors in high-density wards to live with greater dignity and independence.

This platform follows the official Indian colour-coded dustbin system. Every colour is used throughout the design.

  • Green – Biodegradable/Organic
  • Blue – Dry/Recyclable
  • Red – Hazardous/Biomedical
  • Yellow – Sanitary/Medical
  • Black/Grey – General waste
